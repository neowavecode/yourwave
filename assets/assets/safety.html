Child Safety and Protection Standards on YourWave

At YourWave, our priority is to create a safe and respectful environment for all our users. We are firmly committed to combating Child Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (CSEA) and enforce a zero-tolerance policy against any content or behavior that infringes upon the safety of minors.

Although YourWave is designed for users who are 18 years of age or older, we implement rigorous security measures to prevent any form of inappropriate contact or content involving minors. Our goal is to ensure that the platform is never used for illegal or harmful purposes.

---

Our Commitment and Safety Measures

  • Age Restriction: YourWave is an application exclusively for users aged 18 and above. We have verification processes in place to help ensure compliance with this restriction.
  • Content Moderation: We implement both an automated and manual moderation system to quickly detect and remove any content that violates our policies, including explicit, abusive, or exploitative images, audio, or text. Our team actively reviews profiles and interactions.
  • Prevention of Inappropriate Interactions:
    • Private chats are monitored by our automated systems to identify suspicious language or communication patterns.
    • Photos and audio shared are subject to review and may be removed if they violate our guidelines.
    • Geolocation is used exclusively to connect compatible adult users with their consent, without allowing its use for harassment or locating minors.
  • Private Profiles: User profiles are private and only visible within the application to other YourWave users, which limits the exposure of personal information.
  • Staff Training: Our support and moderation team receives ongoing training on child safety policies and protocols for responding to potential CSEA cases.
---

Reporting System and Notification

We encourage all our users to report any content or behavior they find inappropriate, suspicious, or in violation of our policies. Prompt reporting is crucial for our ability to respond effectively.

How to Report a User or Content on YourWave:

  1. From the user's profile: If you find a profile or interaction that concerns you, you can report it directly from their profile within the app.
  2. Select the report type: We offer various reporting categories to help identify the issue:
    • Spam or advertising
    • Inappropriate photo (including any image of exploitation or abuse)
    • Impersonation
    • Harassment or threats
    • Hate speech
    • Illegal activity (including any indication of CSEA)
  3. Provide details: We recommend adding any details or screenshots that may help our team investigate the situation effectively.

Our Response Process to Reports:

All reports are treated with the utmost seriousness and urgency. Our moderation team:

  • Reviews and evaluates each report thoroughly.
  • Acts quickly to remove infringing content, suspend, or terminate accounts of users who violate our policies, especially in CSEA cases.
  • Collaborates with authorities: In cases of suspected CSEA or illegal activity, YourWave will fully cooperate with relevant law enforcement and child protection agencies, providing necessary information for investigation.

Important Note: If you or someone you know has been a victim of CSEA or has information about it, we urge you to contact local authorities or specialized organizations such as the Internet Watch Foundation (IWF) hotline or the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) directly.
